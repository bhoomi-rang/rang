A little about me

Hi, I’m Bhoomi! Each piece is handcrafted by me with care and creativity, bringing warmth and a personal touch to your home. Sharing my love for crochet, I hope my creations add a little joy to your everyday life.
I’m currently studying Communication Design at Atlas Skilltech University, where I enjoy exploring creativity and visual storytelling. Balancing my coursework with everyday adventures, I’m passionate about design, learning new skills, and finding inspiration in the world around me.
At Rang, every stitch is crafted with love, creativity, and care to bring warmth and unique style into your life. Inspired by a passion for handmade artistry, my journey began with a simple hook and a dream to create cozy, beautiful crochet pieces that tell a story and make every customer feel special. Rooted in quality, personal touch, and a drive to inspire, i’m committed to offer one of a kind creations designed to brighten your day and add comfort to your world.
with love, Bhoomi.
